About Me

The day before my 14th birthday, my mother asked me what I wanted. What she meant to ask me was what gifts I wanted, but I took the question a bit too philosophically. I pondered for a while. What, of anything on Earth, do I really want? That night, I concluded that what I wanted was to make music, so I downloaded MuseScore, back when it was still on its first version. I started writing music for my friends in the trumpet section in my small middle school band, eventually auditioning for music school, taking sound design and music theory in college, and creating music almost every day, eventually finding a comfortable niche in electronic music. I have played in live bands, produced others, as well as recorded, produced, mixed, and mastered my debut solo album in 2025. Now, I'm using the skills I've gathered over the years to help other creatives' visions come to life. My DMs and email are always open. Please, allow me the pleasure of making your vision a reality.
